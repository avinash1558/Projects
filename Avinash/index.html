Project 1

Typing

Touch typing is a method of typing on a keyboard using all your fingers without looking at the keys. It is a skill that requires practice and dedication to master. Touch typing is considered to be more efficient than the hunt-and-peck method, which involves the typist looking at the keyboard and finding each individual key. The goal of touch typing is to increase speed, accuracy and comfort when typing.
